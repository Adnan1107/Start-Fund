About StartFund

StartFund is a platform that connects retail investors with startups seeking funding. Our goal is to provide a streamlined, transparent environment where both parties can collaborate and drive growth together.

We empower entrepreneurs to showcase their innovative ideas to a network of investors, while allowing investors to discover promising startups that align with their investment goals.
